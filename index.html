40% Latency Down
3 Channels Built
+
Problem Statement
  • Customers experienced delayed alerts across Email, SMS, and Push — degrading trust in digital banking
  • No unified notification platform; each channel operated in silos with inconsistent SLAs
  • Lack of observability made it impossible to diagnose and fix alert failures before customer impact
My Role as Product Owner
  • Defined and owned the product vision: a single unified alert platform eliminating channel silos and improving customer trust
  • Built and prioritized the product backlog — wrote epics, user stories, and acceptance criteria for each delivery sprint
  • Acted as voice of the customer in every sprint — ensured features delivered real user value, not just technical capability
  • Tracked delivery KPIs via Power BI and SQL dashboards — used data to reprioritize backlog and course-correct mid-sprint
  • Managed stakeholder communication and sprint reviews — aligned leadership on progress, risk, and release readiness
Outcomes & Impact
  • 40% reduction in alert latency — tied directly to prioritization of performance-focused user stories
  • Unified platform shipped across 3 channels — replaced a fragmented, silo-driven architecture
  • Improved customer trust in digital banking — fewer missed and delayed notifications at scale
  • Real-time KPI dashboards enabled proactive backlog decisions before issues reached customers
